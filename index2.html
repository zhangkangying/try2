About Us

Dewen Florist Studio

Chinese flower arrangement, originated from ancient culture and art, embodies the natural realm pursued by Confucianism, Buddhism and Taoism culture in Chinese philosophy, and integrates the cultural connotation of Chinese poetry and painting. Its style mainly reflects the natural beauty of flowers, the beauty of lines and the beauty of artistic conception.

Elements of flower arrangement: Foothold

There are nine important foothold on the earth, the center point is called "pole", is the core of the earth, but also the mother point of all artistic modeling and life, and the heart of heaven.

Elements of flower arrangement: Flower type

Flower type is an independent shape composed of two main branches on the basis of flower phase. The three main branches are all upright, which is the normal state of flowers and trees, and also the basic state of living things.

Elements of flower arrangement: Flower phase

The opening and closing of the three main branches of Chinese flower arrangement is similar to that of the petals. There is an invisible "center point" between the three main branches, which is the spirit of the flower phase.

Elements of flower arrangement:Flower apparatus

Flower apparatus is the main support and decoration of flower arrangement.

Flower history

Chinese flower arrangement in the Wei Jin Southern and northern Dynasties before the original idea and prototype, in the long years flowing, the flower is like a monument, engraved with heavy historical traces, in the changes of one season after another, narrating the transition of life.

  • Bottle, homophonic with "ping", meaning peace and good luck.
  • The pan, which dates back to the Neolithic Age, became a flower vessel in the Han Dynasty 2,000 years ago.
  • Bowls are often compared to golden houses and bouquets for flowers.
  • Vase flowers originated in Tang Dynasty and flourished in Ming and Qing dynasties.
